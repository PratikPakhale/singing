Who says you can't take part in competitions during lockdown?  In Association with Google Drive and Instagram

Over the past four years, stuMagz has seen many students who are brilliant singers and has also played a role in their growth as singers. It is to give these talented singers a platform during the ongoing Covid-19 crisis, that we have initiated this contest. 

If you are a student who loves singing, then this is THE chance for you to showcase your talent. 

It's simple! Register here and take part in this contest!

The registration is free of cost!

How to take part?

*Register by clicking on 'Register Now'.

*Login with your Instagram Account to Continue..

*Upload a video of yourself singing onto YouTube or on Your Instagra Account.

*After Logging In you'll be promptet about response success. Then shoot a video and upload. If it's a youtube video, tag us in your Instagram Post and share the link.

*Then we will post it on stumagz.com/sosc and we will send you your stuMagz contest link.

*You need to use this link to get loves on the article and these loves on the article will carry 30% weightage for you to get into the next round of the competition.

*Judges' decision will have 70% weightage.

That's it! We will get in touch with you after this, with further details!

Hurry up and register now! Last date for registrations: May 02, 2020.

The panel of judges includes Karthik Kodakandla, singer and music director and Krishna Tejaswi, singer and lead vocalist of the band Jammers and PVNS Rohit, playback singer.

Prizes:

1: Cash Prizes for the Top 3 participants.

2: An opportunity as a playback singer in an album.

3: One original music video featuring the winner.
